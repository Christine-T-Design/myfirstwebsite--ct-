Mission Fit

Fitness App UI Design

Project Overview

Mission Fit will have users complete a simple questionnaire that will allow the app to show them specific workouts that will help them achieve their specific fitness goals. Mission Fit also has friendly challenges to help users stay motivated.

Research

While talking to some potential users, I was able to find out what are some issues for current fitness apps.

Problems

Feeling Isolated When Working Out

“I wish I was more connected with others. Working out alone can get boring really fast.

Providing Inappropriate Workout Advice

“I feel that some of the fitness apps aren’t personalized to what I really want.”

Workouts Are Boring And Repetitive

“Apps can have boring workouts. It makes me want to just quit.”

Time to Design

Here are solutions that I came up with to solve the issues that users have brought up.

Solutions

Challenges with Friends

To keep users connected, you can join challenges and send encouragement to others to keep them motivated!

Questionnaire During Sign Up

The questionnaire that users answer during the sign up process helps personalize the app to what user goals are and interests.

Descriptions and Tags

The description is to help users figure out if this workout is something they are interested in. The tags are to help users find similar workouts.
